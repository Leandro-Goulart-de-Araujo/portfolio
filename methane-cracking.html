Methane Pyrolysis via Catalysis

Project Summary: This section presents ongoing and published efforts focused on methane conversion into hydrogen and solid carbon through catalysis and fluidized-bed reactors, with emphasis on modeling, reaction engineering, and reactor design.

Key Contributions:

  • Co-author of a study on direct biogas reforming in catalytic fluidized-bed reactors
  • Lead author of ongoing works investigating the role of carbon formation during methane pyrolysis in fluidized systems
  • Developed kinetic modeling strategies and conducted CFD simulations of microwave-assisted and conventional heating systems
  • Presented original results at international conferences on fluidized-bed CFD and microwave heating
