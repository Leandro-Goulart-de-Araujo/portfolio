ML in Catalysis

Project Summary: This page highlights key contributions to the application of machine learning in catalysis, including both fundamental and applied aspects. The focus is on kinetic modeling, catalyst performance prediction, and ML-assisted reaction system design.

Key Contributions:

  • Lead author of a comprehensive review in the Chemical Engineering Journal (CEJ) outlining current and emerging ML approaches in catalysis
  • Lead author of a Perspective article (under review) on ML for photocatalytic hydrogen production, addressing the fundamentals of photocatalysis, the use of conventional and data-driven approaches to solve the open challenges in the field, a comparison between photocatalysis and catalysis, as well as discussions on data collection, descriptors, and design strategies

Publications:

  • Recent developments in the use of machine learning in catalysis: A broad perspective with applications in kinetics – CEJ (2025)
  • Artificial Intelligence-Driven Advances in Photocatalytic Hydrogen Production – under review
